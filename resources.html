Game Engine Resources

Unreal Engine

Unreal Engine is a powerful and versatile game development
platform created by Epic Games. It enables developers to
create high-quality 3D games, virtual reality experiences,
and cinematic visuals. Known for its cutting-edge
graphics, real-time rendering, and a wide range of tools,
Unreal Engine supports both novice and professional
developers with its user-friendly interface and
scripting options through Blueprints or C++.

Graphics/Art Resources

Adobe Photoshop

Adobe Photoshop is the industry-leading software for
image editing, graphic design, and digital art. Packed
with advanced tools and features, it enables users to
create stunning visuals, manipulate images with precision,
and unleash their creativity. Whether you're editing
photos, designing graphics, or crafting digital artwork,
Photoshop offers unparalleled versatility and control.

3D Modelling resources

Blender

Blender is a free and open-source 3D creation suite that
supports modeling, sculpting, animation, rendering,
and even video editing. With a robust set of tools and
a thriving community, it's a favorite among artists and
developers for creating everything from stunning
animations to immersive 3D worlds. Its adaptability makes it a
powerful choice for both professionals and beginners
in the world of 3D art.

Audio Resources

Audacity

Audacity is an open-source audio editing software that's
perfect for recording, editing, and enhancing sound.
Whether you're a musician, podcaster, or content creator,
Audacity's intuitive interface and powerful tools allow
you to cut, mix, and refine audio tracks with ease.
It's a versatile choice for both amateurs and professionals
seeking high-quality audio production.

Mods Manager

Nexus Mods Manager

Nexus Mod Manager is a comprehensive tool for managing and
installing mods in your favorite PC games. It simplifies
the process of customizing your gaming experience by
providing an easy-to-use interface to download, activate,
and organize mods. With NMM, gamers can effortlessly
enhance visuals, gameplay, and features while keeping their
mods neatly managed.
